Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Coaches Hub

How much does Coaches Hub cost?

Coaches Hub is currently completely free to use with all features included. We're in a beta phase and focused on providing value to coaches while refining our platform. In the future, we may introduce premium tiers, but early users will always receive special benefits.

Are there any limitations to the free version?

Currently, there are no limitations - you have access to all features including the match organizer, calendar, weather forecasts, AI coaching tools, and player access codes. We want coaches to experience the full potential of our platform.

Can my players access the app too?

Yes! You can create access codes for your players that give them limited access to your coaching portal. They can view your bulletin board, upcoming events, and weather forecasts. It's a great way to communicate with your players without needing another app.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard security measures. Your data is encrypted, and we never share your information with third parties. You can also download your data at any time.

What if I need help using the app?

Drop us an email and we are more than happy to assist. There is also a handy in app guide.
