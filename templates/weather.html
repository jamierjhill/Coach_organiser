🎾 Tennis Conditions Guide

Excellent Perfect for tennis: Ideal temperature (12-28°C), low wind (<12 km/h), no rain, comfortable humidity
Good Good for tennis: Minor weather factors like light wind or slightly warm/cool temperatures
Fair Playable but challenging: Windy conditions affect ball control, light rain possible, or temperature extremes
Poor Not recommended: Heavy rain (wet courts), very windy (>30 km/h), too hot (>35°C) or too cold (<5°C)
🏆 Tennis Weather Tips:
  • Wind: Even light wind (10-15 km/h) can affect ball trajectory - adjust your game accordingly
  • Humidity: High humidity (>75%) makes courts play slower and balls feel heavier
  • Temperature: Balls bounce higher in warm weather and lower in cold weather
  • Sun: Check hourly forecasts for sun position - avoid scheduling during peak glare times
  • Rain: Even light drizzle can make courts slippery - wait 30+ minutes after rain stops
